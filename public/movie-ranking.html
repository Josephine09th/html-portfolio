The Best Movie According to Josephine

My top 3 movies of all time.

Passion of Christ

It is about the journey of Jesus Christ from the Garden of Gethsemane to Golgotha.
This movie never failed to make me cry for it shows God's great love for us through
His passion. It has recently been replaced as a top box office of all time, but Jesus
didn't came to be on top of rating, but to show us His love.

The Day After Tomorrow

This is a typical alien invasion type of movie, but the main protagonist has gotten the
chance to reset a day before he got his power. Why I never get tired of watching this
movie is because the lead work hard to have the skills he need to win the invasion and
also knew that he needed support to accompish what is needed to be done. He is not
perfect character,but eventually, he was fully committed to his goal.

Pride and Prejudice

This is my favorite romantic movie. The actors played the role and the lines so well
that they made the book look so interesting that I bought the book, but like the movie better.
The movie is like you are doing a picture reading. I also loved the places were the movie
was filmed. Hopefully, I get to see it in person too.

These are just a few of my favorite movies that I like. Hope you find it interesting too.
